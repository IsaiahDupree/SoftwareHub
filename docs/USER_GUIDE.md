# SoftwareHub User Guide

---

## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Visit the SoftwareHub website
2. Click **Sign In** in the top navigation
3. Enter your email address
4. Check your email for a **magic link** (no password required)
5. Click the link to sign in

Your account is created automatically on first sign-in.

### Your Dashboard

After signing in, you'll be taken to your dashboard at `/app`. Here you can:

- View your purchased software packages
- Access your license keys
- Download software
- Track your course progress
- Manage your profile

---

## Purchasing Software

### Individual Packages

1. Browse the **Packages** page to see available software
2. Click on a package to view details, features, and pricing
3. Click **Buy Now** to proceed to checkout
4. Complete payment through Stripe (credit/debit card)
5. After payment, your license key is generated instantly

### Bundles

Bundles let you purchase multiple packages at a discount:

1. Visit the **Bundles** section
2. Review which packages are included and the savings
3. Click **Get Bundle** to checkout
4. You'll receive separate license keys for each included package

### Subscriptions

Subscription plans give you access to all packages:

1. Visit the **Pricing** page
2. Choose monthly or yearly billing
3. Subscribe through Stripe
4. Access all packages immediately
5. Manage your subscription from the account page

### Promo Codes

If you have a promo code:

1. Enter it during checkout
2. The discount will be applied automatically
3. The final price reflects the discount

## Managing Licenses

### Viewing Your Licenses

1. Go to **My Licenses** from the dashboard
2. Each license shows:
   - Package name and icon
   - License status (active, expired, suspended)
   - Number of active devices out of maximum allowed
   - Expiration date (if applicable)

### Revealing Your License Key

License keys are masked for security. To see the full key:

1. Go to **My Licenses**
2. Click **Reveal Key** next to the license
3. The full key is shown temporarily
4. Copy it and paste into your application

### License Key Format

```
XXXX-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX
```

Example: `ABCD-EFGH-JKLM-NPQR`

### Activating on a Device

1. Install the software on your device
2. Launch the application
3. When prompted, paste your license key
4. Click **Activate**
5. The app will confirm activation

Each license allows activation on a limited number of devices (typically 2).

### Deactivating a Device

If you need to move your license to a new device:

1. Go to **My Licenses** on the web portal
2. Find the license and view active devices
3. Click **Deactivate** next to the device you want to remove
4. You can now activate on a new device

Alternatively, deactivate from within the desktop application if it provides that option.

## Downloading Software

### From the Dashboard

1. Go to your dashboard
2. Find the package you want to download
3. Click **Download**
4. The latest version will download to your computer

### From a Course

If your package is linked to a course:

1. Open the course
2. Navigate to the **Installation** lesson
3. Click the download button within the lesson
4. Follow the installation instructions provided

### System Requirements

- **macOS:** Version 14.0 or later (varies by package)
- **Disk Space:** At least 500MB free
- **Internet:** Required for activation

## Free Trials

Some packages offer free trials:

1. Visit the package page
2. Click **Start Free Trial**
3. You'll receive a temporary license key
4. The trial lasts for the specified period (e.g., 14 days)
5. After the trial, purchase a full license to continue using the software

Trial limitations:
- One trial per package per user
- Trial license key works like a regular key but expires

## FAQ

### I lost my license key. How do I find it?

Go to **My Licenses** and click **Reveal Key** to see the full key.

### Can I use my license on multiple computers?

Yes, each license typically allows 2 devices. You can deactivate old devices to free up slots.

### My license says "expired." What do I do?

Check your **Licenses** page for renewal options. If you have a subscription, verify your payment method is current.

### The app says "Device limit reached." How do I fix this?

1. Go to **My Licenses** on the web portal
2. Deactivate a device you're no longer using
3. Activate on your new device

### I'm having trouble installing the software.

1. Ensure your macOS version meets the minimum requirement
2. If you see "unidentified developer," go to System Preferences > Security & Privacy > General and click "Open Anyway"
3. Try re-downloading the latest version
4. Contact support if the issue persists

### How do I cancel my subscription?

1. Go to your account settings
2. Click **Manage Subscription**
3. You'll be taken to the Stripe customer portal
4. Click **Cancel Subscription**
5. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period

### How do I get a refund?

Contact support with your purchase details. Refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis.

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| Can't sign in | Check your email spam folder for the magic link |
| Magic link expired | Request a new one — links expire after 1 hour |
| Download not starting | Check your browser's download settings; try a different browser |
| App crashes on launch | Delete preferences and reinstall; check system requirements |
| "Invalid license key" | Verify you copied the entire key including dashes |
| "License is suspended" | Contact support for assistance |
| Slow video playback | Check your internet connection; try a lower quality setting |
